HOW TO PLAY

To play Hacker Hangman, first you must create a username for your session.

Next, choose a category for the words you want to guess.

Now, the gameplay screen will have appeared. To play the game, you guess which letters are in the hidden word by clicking them, until the whole word/sentence has been completely guessed. But be careful: if you guess more than 7 times, you will get caught!
